-Drink for 10 seconds every time your parents mention how successful your other siblings are.

-Take an awkwardly long sip of your beer every time your grandpa smirks after making a slightly racist comment about why “dark meat just isn’t as good as white meat.”

-Ripanother shot and pour one for the people next to you if your uncle responds with “Trump’s gonna make America great again damnit.”

Happy drinking.

Being a Short Guy in College: Fuck It

Much like vacuums, prostitutes and Nickelback, being a short guy in college sucks

If you’re a tall guy the world is your oyster. You know what screw that, the world is your world. Tall guys just have it all, endless possibilities.

Let’s imagine you walk into a bar as a tall dude…

(Disclaimer: as a 5’8” guy this is all just speculation for me.)

So you walk in. Everyone sees you right away and all the girls are mesmerized and say hey. Even the dudes walk up and show their appreciation. You easily maneuver through the huge crowd to the bar, where the bartender immediately asks you what you want, ignoring everyone around you like Denny’s employees at corporate ignoring the fact they have tilapia on the menu (no one fucking goes to Denny’s for seafood, this isn’t Singapore).

Every girl you meet wants you more than Lance Armstrong wants a second testicle. When you walk up to a girl she grabs you and takes you right into the bathroom. Inside a stall she does stuff that makes you happier than when a teenage white kid discovers his parents getting divorced means double the presents.

After that she invites you back to her apartment to have a Catholic schoolgirl themed threesome with her model roommate, who only speaks Spanish and is kinkier than Monica Lewinsky on President’s Day.

Now let’s imagine you’re a short guy walking into a bar…

Check that, the bouncer assumed you were under age and didn’t let you in.

Sucks.

Just for kicks though, let’s say you make it into the bar. You see a girl you know and she comes up and talks to you (don’t get cocky shorty, it’s just because short guys are ‘approachable’, or at least a girl told me that when I was trying to think of anything beneficial to being a short guy in college – which is sad really).

So you go up to the bar to get a drink for both of you guys. After 15 minutes of getting elbowed, shoved and engulfed by a crowd more than Colin Kaepernick would be at a white supremacist rally, you finally make it to the bar.

After 10 more minutes of profusely gesturing and hollering at the bartender, you get those couple drinks you ordered.

But now you have to get back to the girl.

The haj back is just as frustrating as the one to the bar, but now you have open drinks that are periodically spilling on you. It’s all worth it though when you finally get back to her.

You can’t find her, but you keep looking around. Ooh there she is coming out of the bathroom. Wait what? Who’s that dude that just came out with her? What the fuck… It’s one of your good friends.

So you just stand there, as immobile as Stephen hawking in a small ditch. They walk by you and that tall piece of shit says, “yo I’m going to go have a threesome with this chick and her Hispanic roommate.”

Fuck.

And there’s a night in the life of a short guy in college. Was it a PSA? Who knows, but if ya don’t know, now you know, tall people.
