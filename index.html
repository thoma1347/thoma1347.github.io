About Me

I grew up in, Winnetka, Ill. a small northern suburb of Chicago and went to New Trier High School. I have two brothers, Evan and Trevor. Evan recently graduated from the University of Iowa and has gone into the real estate business, working in downtown Chicago at @Properties. Trevor is a senior at the University of California Los Angeles majoring in math and statistics. My mom works off and on doing business consulting and my dad recently retired from his job as the VP of menu management at McDonalds.

I’m halfway through my junior year at the University of Miami, where I’m majoring in broadcast journalism, while minoring in sports administration and geography. I’ve been announcing UM sports for the student radio station, WVUM, since the beginning of freshmen year. I’m very passionate about sports, whether it’s writing, watching, playing or announcing. My goal is make it in the sports media industry either as an analyst or a play-by-play announcer. My dream job would be announcing for the Cleveland Indians.
